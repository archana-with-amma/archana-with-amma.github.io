How To

Welcome to Archana By Heart! This will help you find where you make mistakes while doing archana.

How does it work? It's simple. When you start you will see the first verse. State the following verse and click Next. The following verse will be shown. Now mark if the verse you see matches the verse you remembered. Right (green) or Wrong (red). If you need to go back, you can undo by clicking the yellow button or swipe left on the screen if you're on a phone.

The REALLY COOL thing is that once you go through memorize once, it knows what you missed. Next time you do either learn or memorize, you will see the verses COLORED . Red means you got the NEXT verse wrong so go slow .

After you are done. You can generate a quizlet deck. You don't need a quizlet account, however if you have one, feel free to copy the deck to your own account.

If you are on a computer - be sure to checkout the keyboard shortcuts. Simply type ? (Shift + /) to see avilable shortcuts.
